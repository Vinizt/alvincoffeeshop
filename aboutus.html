About Us

At Alvin's Coffee, we take immense pride in our rich Philippine culture and artistry. Our brand is a vibrantcelebration of the deep love we have for our country, reflected in the exceptional coffee and unparalleled servicewe provide. Founded by Steve Benitez on June 28, 1996, Alvin's Coffee stands as a beacon, redefining the landscapefor startup businesses and social enterprises. We are on a mission to shine the global spotlight on the exquisiteflavors of Philippine Coffee and preserve the essence of Filipino tradition. Join us in savoring the perfect blend ofculture, tradition, and unparalleled coffee experiences at Alvin's Coffee.

We put the spotlight on Philippine Coffee.

Each cup of coffee sold supports local farmers through fair trade and contains beans sourced across the Philippines from Sagada & Benguet in the North, to Mt. Apo, Mt. Kitanglad, and Mt. Matutum in the South.

We take Pride in our roots through the Philippine Brand of Hospitality.

Beyond its products, the brand displays the hospitable Filipino Spirit in its stores – making its patrons feel welcome in a place they can call home. We practice the panunumpa, a gesture that stands for service that comes from the heart.
